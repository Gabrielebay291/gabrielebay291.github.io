📸 Photobox-Book-To-PDF - Save Your Photo Books as PDFs

Download Software for Windows

Photobox-Book-To-PDF helps you save your digital photo books as high-resolution files. This tool captures each page of your book and joins them into a single PDF document. The final file keeps the quality of your images for printing or long-term storage.

🚀 How It Works

Many online photo book services keep your books locked within their websites. This software acts as a helper that visits the Photobox page for you. It takes a clear picture of every page and combines them into one document that you can keep on your computer.

💻 System Requirements

To run this tool on your Windows computer, you need the following:

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11.
  • An active internet connection to see your photo book online.
  • A modern web browser like Chrome or Edge.
  • At least 500MB of free disk space.

📥 Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up the software on your Windows computer:

  1. Visit the official download page to obtain the latest version of the application.
  2. Locate the downloaded file in your browser's download folder.
  3. Double-click the file to start the installation process.
  4. Follow the prompts on the screen to finish the setup.

📖 How to Create Your PDF

Once you finish the installation, you can create your first PDF file:

  1. Open your web browser and navigate to your Photobox photo book project.
  2. Copy the website address (URL) from the address bar at the top of your browser.
  3. Open the Photobox-Book-To-PDF application on your desktop.
  4. Paste the address into the box provided in the application window.
  5. Click the Start button to begin the capture process.
  6. Wait while the application visits each page. You will see progress updates on your screen.
  7. Select a folder on your computer when asked where to save the final PDF file.

🛡️ Privacy and Safety

This software operates locally on your machine. Your photos remain on your computer and are not uploaded to any outside servers. The tool only reads the page data to generate the PDF output for your own use.

🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Problems

If you encounter issues, check these common items:

  • Ensure you are logged into your Photobox account in your web browser.
  • Check your internet speed; a slow connection might pause the image capture process.
  • Make sure your firewall allows the application to access the internet.
